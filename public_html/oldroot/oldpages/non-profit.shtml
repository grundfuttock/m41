I have a strong belief in "fairness".
I believe that everyone should be treated equally whether by the private or public sector.

With more and more services being delivered online, there is a significant section of the population who are being excluded. At the same time there is huge growth in low-cost devices capable of accessing those services (tablets and Smartphones).

I am currently helping Trafford Libraries improve their digital inclusivity.

In early 2014 I proposed a new service to help people make use of smartphones and tablet PCs. I set up the service, deliver it, and coordinate other volunteers. This has now expanded to cover a variety of topics all related to helping and motivating people to get the benefits that the internet offers.

I am interested in expanding this work. I can offer non-profit organisations :

  • Advice sessions to clients on topics such as Introductions to
    • Smartphones or tablet PCs
    • Windows Desktops / Laptops
    • E-mail
    • Searching and using the web
    • Online safety
  • Help to provide volunteers with the confidence to deliver the above sessions
  • Background on government's "Digital by Design" strategy
  • Background on the digitally excluded
  • The consultancy services offered elsewhere on this site (Web development etc).
  • A professional approach to reaching your goals
